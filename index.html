Privacy Policy for Mockin: AI Coach

Effective Date: September 8, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Mockin: AI Coach ("Mockin", "we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

Account Information

  • Email address (required for authentication)
  • Name (optional)
  • Profile information you choose to provide
  • Authentication data from third-party sign-in services (Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In)

Content and Communications

  • Conversation history with AI personas in all modes (Interview, Dating, Character)
  • Voice recordings (temporarily processed for transcription only)
  • Text messages and responses
  • Resume documents (if uploaded for job discovery features)
  • Job preferences and search criteria
  • Cover letters generated or edited

Usage Information

  • Practice session dates, duration, and frequency
  • Selected personas and conversation topics
  • Achievement badges earned
  • Feature usage patterns

Information Collected Automatically

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • App version

Analytics Data

  • App performance data
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Error logs and crash reports

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  1. Provide Core Services
    • Enable AI-powered conversations across all modes
    • Process voice input and generate responses
    • Save your progress and conversation history
    • Match you with relevant job opportunities
  2. Improve Our Services
    • Enhance AI response quality
    • Develop new features
    • Fix bugs and improve performance
    • Personalize your experience
  3. Communication
    • Send important service updates
    • Respond to support requests
    • Notify you of new features (with your consent)

Data Processing and Storage

AI Processing

  • Conversations are processed by OpenAI's API to generate responses
  • Voice recordings are transcribed using speech recognition services
  • No voice audio is permanently stored after transcription
  • Conversation data is used only to maintain context during your session

Data Storage

  • User profiles and preferences: Stored securely in Firebase
  • Conversation history: Stored on your device and in our secure cloud database
  • Resume data: Encrypted and stored securely when uploaded
  • All data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols

Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Conversation history: Retained for your reference until manually deleted
  • Voice recordings: Processed immediately and not retained
  • Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymized after 90 days

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  1. Service Providers
    • OpenAI (for AI processing)
    • Firebase (for authentication and data storage)
    • Analytics providers (anonymized data only)
  2. Legal Requirements
    • If required by law or legal process
    • To protect our rights or safety
    • To prevent fraud or illegal activities
  3. Business Transfers
    • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Your Rights and Choices

Data Access and Control

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your conversation history

Opt-Out Options

  • Analytics: Disable analytics collection in app settings
  • Communications: Unsubscribe from promotional emails
  • Voice Processing: Use text-only mode

Account Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Go to Settings in the app
  2. Select "Delete Account"
  3. Confirm your choice

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure development practices

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
